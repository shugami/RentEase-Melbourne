About RentEase Melbourne

Learn about our mission to provide affordable housing solutions across Victoria

Who We Are

RentEase Melbourne is a Victorian non-profit organization established in 2010 with a clear mission: to connect Australians with affordable housing options and advocate for housing equity across Melbourne and Victoria.

As housing affordability continues to be a significant challenge in Melbourne's competitive rental market, our team of dedicated housing professionals works tirelessly to bridge the gap between renters and quality, affordable housing options.

We believe that safe, secure, and affordable housing is a fundamental right for all Victorians. Through our comprehensive property listings, support services, and educational resources, we help individuals and families navigate the complex rental landscape and find homes that meet their needs and budgets.

Based in Melbourne's CBD, our organization serves communities across all of Melbourne's diverse suburbs, from the inner city to the outer growth corridors. We work closely with property owners, government agencies, and community organizations to increase the availability of affordable rental options and provide support to those facing housing challenges.

Our Mission

RentEase Melbourne exists to create pathways to affordable, secure housing for all Victorians through innovative solutions, community partnerships, and advocacy.

We are committed to:

  • Connecting renters with affordable housing options across Melbourne and Victoria
  • Providing education and resources on tenant rights and responsibilities under Victorian law
  • Advocating for policy changes that increase housing affordability and security
  • Supporting vulnerable communities, including low-income earners, students, seniors, and new migrants
  • Collaborating with property owners to expand affordable rental opportunities

Our Values

  • Accessibility

    We believe everyone deserves access to safe, secure, and affordable housing regardless of their background or circumstances.

  • Community

    We value the diverse communities of Melbourne and work to strengthen connections between renters, property owners, and support services.

  • Equity

    We are committed to addressing systemic barriers in the housing market and promoting fair access to rental opportunities.

  • Innovation

    We continuously seek creative solutions to Melbourne's housing challenges through technology, partnerships, and new approaches.

  • Integrity

    We operate with transparency, honesty, and accountability in all our interactions with clients, partners, and the community.

Our Melbourne Journey

The story of RentEase Melbourne and our impact on Victoria's housing landscape

2010

Foundation

RentEase Melbourne was founded by a group of housing advocates and community leaders in response to Melbourne's growing housing affordability crisis. Our initial focus was on connecting low-income earners with affordable rental options in the inner suburbs.

2013

Expansion to Outer Suburbs

As Melbourne's housing market continued to tighten, we expanded our services to include the outer growth corridors, recognizing the need for affordable housing options across the metropolitan area.

2015

Launch of Tenant Support Services

We introduced comprehensive tenant support services, including education on Victorian rental laws, assistance with rental applications, and advocacy for tenants facing housing challenges.

2018

Digital Platform Launch

Our online platform was launched, making it easier for Melburnians to search for affordable rental properties and access housing resources from anywhere in Victoria.

2020

COVID-19 Response

During the pandemic, we pivoted to provide emergency housing support and rental assistance to Victorians affected by lockdowns and economic hardship, helping thousands of families maintain stable housing.

2023

New Initiatives

Today, we continue to expand our services with innovative programs like our International Student Housing Support, Rental Guarantee Program, and Housing Advocacy Network across Melbourne.
