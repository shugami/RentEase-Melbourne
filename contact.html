Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I apply for a rental property?

To apply for a rental property, you can fill out an application form online through our website or visit our office in person. You'll need to provide identification, proof of income, and references.

What types of rental assistance do you offer?

As a non-profit organization, we offer various forms of rental assistance including help with security deposits, application fee waivers for qualifying individuals, and guidance on government housing programs.

How long does the application process take?

The application process typically takes 2-3 business days, which includes background checks, credit checks, and contacting references. We strive to make the process as efficient as possible.

Do you charge an application fee?

Yes, there is a standard application fee of $25 per applicant to cover the cost of background and credit checks. However, this fee may be waived for qualifying low-income applicants.
