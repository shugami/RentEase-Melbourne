Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about student housing in Melbourne

What documents do I need to apply for student housing?

You'll typically need proof of identity, proof of income or scholarship, student ID, rental references (if available), and bank statements. International students may also need their visa documentation.

How much should I budget for student accommodation?

Budget varies by location and property type. Shared houses typically range from $150-300 per week, while studio apartments can cost $250-450 per week. Remember to factor in utilities, internet, and bond payment.

Can international students apply for bond loans?

Yes, international students can apply for bond loans through various programs. Our team can help you understand your eligibility and guide you through the application process.

What's the best time to look for student housing?

The best time is 6-8 weeks before semester starts. January-February and June-July are peak periods, so start your search early to have more options.
